Quick Notes on My Resume

Resumes are a funny thing; you try to condense the essence of your accomplishments to a single page or two. An overwhelmed hiring manager or an algorithm sorts through your material, and if you have presented your value proposition well enough, you might begin the interview process.

I have capitulated to this song and dance, but I believe there is substantially more to matching people with their suitable positions than the current process allows. If you are interested in getting to know me, please instead of combing through my resume, get in touch through LinkedIn, read my articles, and forge a connection with me. We'll get a better sense of whether or not we're right for each other; I guarantee it.
